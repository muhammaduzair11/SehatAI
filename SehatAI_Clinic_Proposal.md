# SehatAI Voice Assistant for Clinics

## One-Line Value Proposition
A friendly, always-available voice assistant that answers patient calls, books appointments, and supports your clinic—day and night.

---

## The Problem Clinics Face
- Missed calls from patients, especially during busy hours or after closing
- Reception staff overwhelmed by constant phone calls and multitasking
- Patients unable to book or confirm appointments outside clinic hours
- Lost opportunities for new and returning patients

---

## The Solution: Voice AI Assistant
- SehatAI answers every call to your clinic, any time of day
- Listens to patient requests and books appointments directly into your schedule
- Answers common questions about timings, location, and services
- Transfers urgent calls to staff when needed
- Ensures no patient call goes unanswered

---

## How It Works (Simple Flow)
1. Patient calls your clinic number
2. SehatAI answers with a friendly greeting
3. Listens and understands the patient’s request
4. Books an appointment, answers a question, or transfers the call if urgent
5. Confirms the action with the patient before ending the call

---

## Key Features
- Appointment booking and confirmations
- Answers common patient questions (timings, address, services)
- Transfers urgent or complex calls to clinic staff
- Available 24/7, including weekends and holidays
- Customizable greetings and responses for your clinic

---

## Benefits for the Clinic
- Happier patients who always reach a helpful voice
- Less stress and workload for reception staff
- Fewer missed appointments and lost patients
- More time for staff to focus on in-person care
- Potential for higher revenue through better patient retention

---

## Real-Life Use Cases
- A patient calls at 9pm to book an appointment for the next day—SehatAI handles it instantly
- An emergency call is recognized and transferred to the on-call doctor or staff
- Routine questions about clinic hours or directions are answered without staff involvement

---

## What the Assistant Does Not Do
- Does not provide any medical diagnosis or treatment advice
- Never replaces the expertise of your doctors or staff
- Always forwards complex or sensitive cases to a human team member
